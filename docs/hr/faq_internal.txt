Internal Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q1: What are the standard working hours for the office?
A1: Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday, including a one-hour lunch break.


Q2: How do I apply for annual or sick leave?
A2: Leave requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal at least two weeks in advance for planned absences.


Q3: Who should I contact for technical issues or IT support?
A3: Please raise a ticket via the IT Helpdesk portal or email support@internal.com for any hardware or software assistance.


Q4: What is the process for claiming travel reimbursements?
A4: Employees should submit their expense claims with original receipts via the Finance module within 30 days of travel.


Q5: How do I report a security incident or lost ID card?
A5: Immediately notify the Security Operations Center (SOC) and your direct manager to ensure access is restricted.


Q6: Where can I find the latest copy of the general company policies?
A6: All official policy documents are hosted in the "Policies" folder on the company intranet's main landing page.


Q7: How often are performance reviews conducted?
A7: Formal performance reviews occur twice a year, consisting of a mid-year check-in and an annual year-end evaluation.


Q8: What is the formal grievance procedure?
A8: Grievances should first be discussed with your manager; if unresolved, a formal report can be filed with the HR Relations team.


Q9: Are there any mandatory trainings I need to complete?
A9: Yes, all employees must complete the annual Compliance, Cybersecurity Awareness, and Workplace Safety modules.


Q10: How can I update my personal details such as address or bank info?
A10: You can update your contact information and banking details directly under the "My Profile" section in the HRIS system.
